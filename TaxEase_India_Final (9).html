Key PPF Facts
Tax Free (EEE Category) — Investment, interest earned, and maturity amount are all 100% tax free. No tax at any stage.
Government Backed — Completely safe investment, fully guaranteed by the Government of India. Zero risk of loss.
Lock-in Period: 15 Years — Premature closure not allowed. Partial withdrawal permitted from Year 7 onwards once per year.
Section 80C Deduction — PPF investment qualifies for tax deduction up to ₹1,50,000 per financial year under Section 80C.
Loan Facility — You can avail a loan against your PPF account balance from the beginning of Year 3 up to the end of Year 6.
